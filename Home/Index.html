About Us

We pledge to provide regal, personalised service and facilities for all our guests who are welcomed, with arms wide open to a majestic , exclusive, luxurious refined ambience.The Seaside Park,beach experience rejuvenates the heart, body and soul, where guests experience the unexpected.

Discover Our Rooms

Enjoy coastal views from your private balcony in each of our modernly designed contemporary rooms. Choose from plush ensuite single and double rooms in fresh, neutral tones, or quietly grand suites with an extra large private terrace. Be pampered with a wide range of facilities, 24-hour room service, and free, fast wifi.

Complete seclusion, privacy and exclusive luxury are the hallmarks of the VIP wing at Sea Side South Park. The VIP wing has three highly celebrated, beautifully decorated Ambassador Suites styled with superb art works, as well as the exclusive Presidential Suite. Feel completely at home with your own entrance, private terrace and parking space.
